The Complete Guide to Migrating from Shared Hosting Without Losing Rankings

Migrating from shared hosting to a more robust solution requires careful planning to maintain SEO rankings and traffic. This guide outlines essential steps, from creating comprehensive backups to monitoring post-migration performance. Key tasks include selecting the right hosting provider, properly transferring files and databases, preserving SEO elements, and implementing necessary redirects. The process emphasizes maintaining URL structures, updating DNS settings, and thorough testing across devices. Success depends on careful preparation, including documenting current configurations, performing migrations during low-traffic periods, and having a rollback plan ready. Post-migration monitoring ensures sustained performance and rankings.
