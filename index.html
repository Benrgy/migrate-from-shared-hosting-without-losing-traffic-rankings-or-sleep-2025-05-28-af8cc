Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the migration process take?

Most migrations are completed within 24-48 hours. Small sites can be migrated in as little as 6 hours, while larger e-commerce sites may take up to 72 hours for full optimization.

Will my site experience any downtime?

No. Our zero-downtime migration process uses advanced cloning and synchronization technology to ensure your site remains accessible throughout the entire migration.

How do you maintain SEO rankings during migration?

We preserve all SEO elements including meta data, URL structures, and redirects. Our proprietary SEO protection system ensures search engines correctly index your new site location.

What support is available during migration?

Our expert support team is available 24/7 via live chat and email. We also provide emergency phone support for critical issues during migration.

What happens if something goes wrong?

Our system creates multiple backup points during migration. If any issues occur, we can instantly rollback to any previous state, ensuring your site's safety.
