Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the migration process

How long does the migration process take?

Most migrations complete within 24-48 hours. Small sites can be done in 6 hours, while large e-commerce sites may take up to 72 hours for full optimization. We work around the clock to minimize timing.

Will my site experience any downtime?

Absolutely not. Our zero-downtime migration uses advanced cloning and synchronization technology. Your site remains fully accessible throughout the entire process with automatic rollback protection.

How do you maintain SEO rankings during migration?

We preserve all SEO elements including meta data, URL structures, redirects, and internal linking. Our proprietary SEO protection system ensures search engines correctly index your new location without ranking loss.

What support is available during migration?

Our expert support team is available 24/7 via live chat, email, and emergency phone support. You'll have a dedicated migration specialist assigned to your project from start to finish.

What happens if something goes wrong?

Our system creates multiple backup points during migration. If any issues occur, we can instantly rollback to any previous state within minutes. Your site's safety is our top priority.
